FHA loans are one of the most popular options for California first-time buyers. With low down payment requirements and flexible guidelines, FHA programs help thousands of families qualify every year.

Benefits of an FHA Loan

  • Only 3.5% down payment required
  • Flexible credit score requirements
  • Competitive interest rates
  • Gift funds allowed for down payment
  • More forgiving debt-to-income ratios
