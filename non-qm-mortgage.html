Flexible Mortgage Solutions for Self-Employed & Investors

Not every borrower fits inside the traditional mortgage box — and that’s where Non-QM (Non-Qualified Mortgage) programs shine. If you’re self-employed, own multiple businesses, or invest in rental properties, Trusted Statewide offers solutions designed to work with your unique income situation.

Our Non-QM Programs

  • Bank Statement Loans – Qualify using 12–24 months of bank statements instead of tax returns.
  • DSCR Investor Loans – Qualify based on property cash flow, not personal income.
  • Asset Depletion Loans – Use your liquid assets to demonstrate ability to repay.
  • 1099 Income Loans – Designed for independent contractors and gig workers.
  • Interest-Only & Expanded DTI Options – Perfect for maximizing cash flow.

Who Benefits from Non-QM Loans

Entrepreneurs, real estate investors, business owners, and retirees often use Non-QM programs to qualify for financing that traditional lenders might decline. If you have strong credit and income but a unique financial profile, this could be your ideal solution.
