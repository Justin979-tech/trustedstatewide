Unlock Your Home’s Equity with Confidence

A reverse mortgage allows homeowners 62 and older to tap into the equity they’ve built — without giving up home ownership. At Trusted Statewide, I help California seniors create financial flexibility and enjoy peace of mind in retirement.

Reverse Mortgage Benefits

  • Stay in your home while accessing tax-free funds
  • Eliminate monthly mortgage payments (borrower remains responsible for taxes and insurance)
  • Flexible payout options — lump sum, line of credit, or monthly income
  • Protection under FHA’s Home Equity Conversion Mortgage (HECM) program
  • Trusted, personalized service — I’ll guide you through every step

Who Qualifies for a Reverse Mortgage?

Reverse mortgages are available to homeowners who:

  • Are 62 years of age or older
  • Own and occupy their home as a primary residence
  • Have sufficient home equity and maintain the property

If you or your loved ones are exploring this option, I can help assess eligibility and find the right program for your goals.
