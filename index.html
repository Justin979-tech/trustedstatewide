Mortgage FAQs – Trusted Statewide

What credit score do I need to buy a home in California?

Most conventional loans require a 620+ credit score, FHA loans may allow approval with scores as low as 580, and jumbo loans often require stronger credit. I’ll review your situation and match you with the right option.

How much down payment is required for a home loan?

Conventional loans may allow as little as 3% down, FHA loans 3.5%, VA loans 0% down, and jumbo loans typically 10–20% down. I’ll help you structure the right down payment strategy for your needs.

Can I qualify for a jumbo loan in San Francisco, Los Angeles, or San Diego?

Yes. I work with jumbo and super-jumbo loan programs across California’s high-value markets. Whether you’re buying in San Francisco, Los Angeles, or San Diego, I’ll tailor jumbo financing solutions to your property and profile.

Do you offer VA loans for veterans and active-duty service members?

Yes. VA loans provide eligible veterans and service members with 0% down financing and competitive terms. I specialize in guiding California veterans through a seamless VA loan process.

Can seniors in California qualify for a reverse mortgage?

Yes. Reverse mortgages are available to homeowners 62 and older who want to access equity without selling their home. I’ll walk you through your options and help you use your home’s value to improve cash flow.

Do you work with self-employed borrowers?

Yes. For business owners, entrepreneurs, and investors, I offer Non-QM solutions like bank statement loans and DSCR loans. These allow qualified borrowers to secure financing without traditional W-2 income documentation.

Can I apply online and get pre-approved quickly?

Yes. Trusted Statewide offers a seamless, touchless online application process. You can securely upload documents and receive a fast pre-approval, often the same day. Start your application now.
