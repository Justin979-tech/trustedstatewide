California Mortgage Tips

Here’s what every California buyer or homeowner should know:

  • Loan limits vary widely across counties — jumbo loans are common in high-cost areas.
  • Refinancing can reduce payment OR tap equity depending on your goals.
  • DSCR loans allow investors to qualify based on rental income instead of tax returns.
  • Reverse mortgages allow homeowners 62+ to eliminate monthly mortgage payments.
  • Pre-approval strength dramatically improves offer acceptance in competitive markets.
