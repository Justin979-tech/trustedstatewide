The APR represents the total cost of borrowing, including interest and fees. It helps you compare loan options more accurately.

Debt-to-Income Ratio (DTI)

DTI compares your monthly debt payments to your gross monthly income. Lower DTI ratios improve your chance of approval.

Loan-to-Value Ratio (LTV)

LTV is the percentage of the home's price financed with a mortgage. Lower LTV often results in better terms.

Jumbo Loan

A loan amount above conforming limits. Common in California due to high home prices. Learn more in our Jumbo Loan Guide.

Reverse Mortgage

A program for homeowners 62+ that allows access to home equity without monthly payments. See full details at our Reverse Mortgage Page.
